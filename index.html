Mr. Majed Farhan Hamanini Al-Shammari

Director

Mr. Majed F. Hamanini Al-Shammari is the current Director of Light Crude Sales & Negotiations in Saudi Aramco.

From 2018 to 2021 he was Saudi Aramco’s Strategy & Development and Chief Financial Officer. Prior to this he held a number of leadership positions at the company, including Financial Controller, and Treasurer, and formerly served as Chairman of Saudi Aramco Development Company (SADCO) and Chairman of the Wisayah Investment Management Company, and as a member of the Board of Directors of Showa Shell Sekiyu K.K., Sadara Chemical Company, ARLANXEO Holding B.V., and Fujian Refining and Petrochemical Company.

Mr. Al-Shammari earned a bachelor’s degree in Industrial Engineering from the University of Jeddah. He has completed a number of executive leadership programs.
